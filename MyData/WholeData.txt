Abbottabad:
	desc:
		Abbottabad District is a district of the Pakistani province of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.
		It is part of Hazara Division and covers an area of 1,969 km2, with the city of Abbottabad being the principal town.
		The district is named after Major James Abbott, the first deputy commissioner of Hazara.According to the Alif Ailaan Pakistan District Education Rankings 2015,
		Abbottabad is ranked 31 out of 148 districts in terms of education. For facilities and infrastructure, the district is ranked 72 out of 148. The city is also known 
		because of its beauty. The city was also in one of the considerations for the capital city of Pakistan.
	Harnoi:
		desc:

			There is a stream of river that flows from the town and all the way through abbottabad. The river is termed as 'Kunhar River'. The place lies between abbottabad and
			nathiagali.

	Sajikot Waterfall:
		desc:
			Sajikot Waterfall is a waterfall located in Havelian Tehsil, Abbottabad District, in Pakistan's Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Province.
			It is a popular tourist destination in Abbottabad District. It is about 27 kilometres from Havelian and 40 kilometres from Abbottabad District.
			Waterfall is located around 1 hour drive from Havelian and 1.5 hr drive from Abbottabad main city. You need to hike your way down to reach the waterfall.
		extras:
			Hotel and Restaurant accomodation, Swimming, Canteens.
	Thandiani:
		desc:
			Thandiani is a hill station in the Galyat area of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa the Province of Pakistan.
			The place is located in the northeast of Abbottabad District and is about 37.5 kilometres (23.3 mi) from Abbottabad in the foothills of the Himalayas.
			To the east beyond the Kunhar River lies the snow-covered Pir Panjal mountain range of Kashmir.The hills of Thandiani are about 2,750 metres (9,020 ft) above sea level and 
			are mostly covered with snow throughout the winters.
		extras:
			Hotels and Restaurant accomodation, Horse Riding, Camping, Canteens. 
	Umbrella Waterfall:
		desc:
			Umbrella Waterfall is a waterfall located in the Sajikot area of Abbottabad District.
			It has recently emerged as a new tourist attraction in the KPK province of Pakistan. The waterfall is located 27 Kilometers from Havelian.
			The Umbrella Waterfall is on the same road where the famous Sajikot Waterfall is located.
			To reach the waterfall, you have to hike down about 30 to 45 minutes from the village of Poona.
		extras:
			Hotels and Restaurant accomodation, Swimming, Canteens.
